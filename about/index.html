About Me

Hi, I’m Mackie! I’m from Las Cruces, New Mexico and graduated from New Mexico State University in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (concentration in computers and microelectronics.)

On this website, you can see some of the electronics hobby projects I’ve worked on over the past few years– these include PCB design, hardware RE, and video game console portablizing. I enjoy working on lithium-ion systems and voltage regulation, especially when I get the opportunity to use new and interesting ICs in chip-scale packages. I’m anxious to explore FPGAs and HDI PCBs as soon as the silicon shortage abates.

My other interests include political theory, hiking, CRT monitors, PC gaming, and Bronze Age comic books.
