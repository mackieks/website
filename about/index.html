About Me

Hi, I’m Mackie! I’m from Las Cruces, New Mexico and hold a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in computers and microelectronics from NMSU. I currently work on next-generation integrative augmented reality hardware at Magic Leap, Inc.

On this website, you can see some of the electronics hobby projects I’ve worked on over the past few years, spanning PCB design and assembly, microsoldering, hardware RE, and video game console portablizing. I enjoy working with lithium-ion systems, power management, and microcontrollers, especially when I get the opportunity to use new and interesting chip-scale ICs.

My other interests include political theory, hiking, CRT monitors, PC gaming, and Bronze Age comic books.
